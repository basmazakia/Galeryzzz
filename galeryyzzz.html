About Me

My name is Basma, and I am currently a student at UPI Bandung. However, it's important to note that I attend the UPI Cibiru campus, where I am majoring in Multimedia Education. This field perfectly aligns with my interests and allows me to explore various aspects of multimedia.

In the past, I had an intense passion for drawing, and it was a significant part of my life. Although I don't draw as much as I used to, I still find great joy in it whenever I do. My love for art hasn't diminished; it's just that my focus has broadened. Alongside drawing, I have developed a strong interest in designing. Whether it's graphic design, digital art, or other forms of creative expression, I find immense satisfaction in bringing my ideas to life through design.

My interests have always revolved around the realm of art and design. This passion drives me to constantly seek new ways to enhance my skills and knowledge in these areas. Whether I am sketching a new idea or working on a design project, I am always eager to learn and grow as a multimedia artist.
